# PROJECT PRESENTATION CONTENT: CONTEXT, CHALLENGES & SOLUTIONS
## SYSTEM: FPT CLUBS MANAGEMENT

---

## 📋 SLIDE 1: PROJECT CONTEXT
* **Current Operations:** FPT University has dozens of highly active student clubs, but they currently operate via fragmented tools like Google Sheets, personal Facebook/Zalo chats, and emails.
* **Key Administrative Pain Points:** University staff have no visibility into daily club operations; the approval pipeline for events is slow and scattered across departments; club funds and gear are easily lost during board handovers.

---

## ⚡ SLIDE 2: 4 REAL-WORLD OPERATIONAL CHALLENGES
1. **Attendance Fraud & Activity Point Manipulation:** Students upload fake check-in photos or sign in on behalf of others to maintain scholarships and graduation requirements, while club leaders tend to turn a blind eye and approve friends.
2. **Poor Financial Control & Asset Loss:** Club funds are managed via personal bank accounts without transparent receipts, while shared equipment (cameras, speaker sets) is damaged or lost without clear individual liability during leadership transitions.
3. **Passive Membership ("Ghost Members") & Unequal Workload:** Students register for clubs just to boost their CVs but skip meetings and activities; event logistics end up overloading 3-4 core members with no metric tools to filter or remove inactive members.
4. **Schedule Conflicts & Venue Bottlenecks:** Multiple clubs request to book the same hall, lobby, or Alpha room at the same time, leading to last-minute clashes while university staff are overwhelmed by manual room scheduling.

---

## 🎯 SLIDE 3: 4 CORRESPONDING OPERATIONAL SOLUTIONS
1. **Two-Step Evidence Verification Workflow:** Implement a cross-verification pipeline from the Club level (verifying physical presence) to the University level (auditing and locking data) to maintain transparent logs and prevent collusive approvals.
2. **Digital Cashbook & Shared Asset Tracking:** Digitalize all financial records with mandatory upload of transaction invoices; members borrowing club assets must register the equipment's condition on the system to establish personal financial liability.
3. **Contribution Logs & Activity Heatmaps:** Log specific roles and actual working hours for each member per event; this provides club boards with factual data to filter out "ghost members" and reward active contributors fairly.
4. **Smart Scheduler & Automated Venue Locking:** Centralize room and equipment bookings into a single interactive calendar; the system automatically locks occupied slots and allows staff to approve facility requests with a single click.
